Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a decision?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Add your factors (what matters to you), then add your options and score each one against your factors.

How does Collaborate work?

With a Collaborate purchase, you can invite others to score your decision. Each person scores independently, and you can see individual and averaged results.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the Pricing screen and tap "Restore purchases" at the bottom.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data.
